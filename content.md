### Event Logistics

### About the Debate
The Puducherry Youth Policy Debate 2026 is a structured platform where students engage with real-world issues at the intersection of technology, governance, and public policy. As innovation increasingly shapes governance, digital infrastructure, and economic development, engineering students play an important role in understanding how technology influences policy and society. This debate encourages participants to explore these issues through evidence-based arguments, structured dialogue, and critical thinking.

### Debate Motions & Topics
*(Compiled from various promotional materials)*
* **Governance & Politics:**
    * Who Really Governs Puducherry? / Governance in Puducherry: Constructive or Destructive?
    * Young India, Old Parliament? / Youth in parliament: Risk or Opportunity
    * Freebies or Fiscal Burden? / The Politics of Freebies: Welfare or Vote Buying?
* **Technology & Rights:**
    * AI & Privacy: Freedom vs security

### Debate Format
* Opening statements
* Team arguments
* Rebuttal round
* Audience questions
* Closing remarks

### Evaluation Criteria
* Research depth
* Logical clarity
* Rebuttal strength
* Policy insight
* Communication skills

* **Participation:** A "Certificate of Participation" is awarded to participants in recognition of outstanding articulation, critical thinking, and persuasive public speaking, promoting informed engagement with governance, public policy, and democratic dialogue.

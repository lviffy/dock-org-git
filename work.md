# Puducherry Outlook Conclave 2026

## Shaping Conversations. Inspiring Change.

The **Puducherry Outlook Conclave 2026**, organized by the **Democratic Organization for Civic Knowledge (DOCK)**, brought together policymakers, administrators, students, and community leaders to discuss Puducherry's present realities and future opportunities.

Held on **5 February 2026** at **Anandha Inn, Puducherry**, the conclave served as a platform for meaningful civic dialogue, informed discussions, and collaborative thinking toward the region's sustainable development.

---

## About the Event

The conclave focused on understanding Puducherry's governance structure, economic challenges, public services, and development opportunities. Through expert-led discussions and audience engagement, participants explored practical solutions and long-term strategies for the Union Territory's growth.

More than **500 students** from leading educational institutions participated in the event, making it one of the region's notable civic engagement initiatives.

## Key Discussion Themes

### Governance & Accountability

Exploring Puducherry's unique administrative structure, financial realities, and the importance of transparent, citizen-focused governance.

### Education & Healthcare

Discussing innovative approaches to strengthen educational opportunities, healthcare accessibility, and community well-being.

### Economic Development

Identifying growth opportunities through tourism, entrepreneurship, skill development, technology, and business-friendly policies.

### Youth Empowerment

Encouraging young people to become active contributors to Puducherry's future through leadership, innovation, and civic participation.

---

## Distinguished Speakers

### Dr. Kiran Bedi

**Former Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry**

Shared insights on governance, public administration, fiscal responsibility, entrepreneurship, and the role of youth in driving meaningful change.

### Shri G. Theva Neethi Dhas, IAS (Retd.)

**Former Secretary to the Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry**

Discussed Puducherry's historical evolution, constitutional framework, and governance challenges as a Union Territory.

### Shri Jose Charles Martin

**President, Latchiya Jananayaga Katchi**

Presented a vision for Puducherry's future, focusing on infrastructure, employment, public services, economic growth, and youth development.

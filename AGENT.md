# AGENT.md

## Project Name

**Civic Knowledge**

## Project Overview

Civic Knowledge is an NGO-focused digital platform that showcases civic engagement initiatives, governance knowledge, public policy awareness, democratic participation, and citizen empowerment programs.

The platform will also serve as a public-facing showcase for selected achievements, impact stories, and electoral engagement outcomes associated with Dock Consulting. While Civic Knowledge maintains its own NGO identity and mission, it may highlight relevant contributions, case studies, research, and measurable impacts delivered by Dock Consulting.

The website should be designed to communicate trust, transparency, public impact, and civic participation.

---

# Project Objectives

1. Showcase Civic Knowledge's mission, programs, campaigns, and community initiatives.
2. Highlight selected Dock Consulting achievements and contributions.
3. Present impact stories and success narratives.
4. Demonstrate electoral and governance-related impact where appropriate.
5. Support multilingual content delivery in:
   - English
   - Telugu
6. Enable theme-based awareness campaigns.
7. Provide a scalable content structure for future expansion.
8. Maintain accessibility and mobile-first design principles.
